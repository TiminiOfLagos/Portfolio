About me

Design is a blend of creativity and strategy. I practice both.

I'm Oluwadamilola Salami, a UX Product Designer based in Lagos, Nigeria. For the past 4+ years I've helped startups, product teams and AI companies turn complex ideas into digital products that people actually enjoy using.

My work runs across AI, SaaS, Fintech and PropTech. I've led end-to-end design on national-scale AI and data platforms, from language-data infrastructure to public-sector systems, and shipped websites and products for compliance, banking, education and software companies. The people who rely on them are spread across Africa, Europe and North America.

I work end to end: research grounds the thinking, design systems keep it consistent, and craft makes it memorable. And because I build in Framer and Webflow myself, my designs don't stop at mockups. They ship, and I stay around after launch to keep them sharp.

Away from client work I write about design on Medium and share case studies on Behance. If you're building something ambitious, I'd love to hear about it.

01 Quick FAQ
What can I book the strategy call for?

Anything product: a new website in Framer or Webflow, a product design engagement, a UX audit of an existing app, or advice on where to start. It's a free 30-minute Google Meet.

Do you design and build?

Yes. I design in Figma and ship marketing sites myself in Framer or Webflow. For app products, I hand off developer-ready designs and stay close through implementation.

How do you usually work?

Discovery first: I map goals, users and constraints, then move through wireframes, visual design and prototyping in quick feedback loops. You always know what's happening and why.

How fast can we launch?

A focused landing page can go live in 1 to 2 weeks. Full websites and product engagements typically run 3 to 6 weeks depending on scope.

Where are you based, and does timezone matter?

Lagos, Nigeria (WAT). I've worked with teams across Europe, North America and Africa, and overlap has never been a problem.
